Enter the latitude and longitude (in that order, separated by a comma) of the area where you want to place the content into the input field at the top left of the screen, which has `latitude, longitude` as its placeholder.
Then, press the **Set Camera Target** button.

<img width="640" src={CameraTargetImage} />
<br />
<br />

:::tip

To find the latitude and longitude to enter, it is useful to use [Google Maps](https://www.google.co.jp/maps).
Right-clicking on the map will display a menu from which you can select the latitude and longitude values; these values will then be copied to your clipboard.

<img width="160" class="border" src={GoogleMapsMenuImage} />

:::
